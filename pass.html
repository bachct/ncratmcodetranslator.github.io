The standing procedure before reimaging a HDD:
  1. Contact Help Desk to ensure no transactions are queued, if so ask them to turn off validation, (remembering to get it switched back on after the update)
  2. Copy Journal and Customerâ€™s graphics to your USB or USB HDD
  3. To load the new image insert your Acronis Bootable USB key, keyboard and mouse into spare USB ports.
  4. Enter the BIOS by pushing F2 then enter the password Eftex1. Make your USB key the first boot option. You may need to push Shift 1 to enable it.
  5. Exit the BIOS and boot from your USB Acronis key and restore the HDD with the new image. Once it is imaged and you exit Acronis the ATM will restart.
  6. Enter the BIOS and make the HDD the only bootable device. Save these settings.
