  1. Enter Diagnostic FDK.
  2. Press 1 - Configuration.
  3. Press 4 - Configure Comms.
  4. Press 1 - OK to proceed.
  5. Press 1 - TCP/IP Access.
  6. Press 36 - ENTER - To select Protocol.
  8. 2E05 Enter - Connect to port number.
  9. 3C00 Enter - Reconnection timer.
  10. FF Enter - Clockticks.
  11. 04 Enter - Length size.
  12. 02 Enter - Length format.
  13. 04 Enter - Length adjust.
  14. 04 Enter - Rx bytes to remove.
  15. E803 Enter - Local TCP port.
  16. 2 IBM TCP / IP SSE Enter.
  17. 92 Enter - Protocol 92.
  22. Enter - Just hit Enter.
  24. 09 Enter - Hops to Dest Default=1.
  25. Exit.
  26. Press 1 Yes - OK to proceed, ATM will reset twice.

  4. 1 IBM/IP Diags.

  6. Press Print for output.
